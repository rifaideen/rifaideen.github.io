Labaiik

I worked on the APIs, search, and catalog for Labaiik, a popular e-commerce app in the Middle East. I developed and maintained a set of RESTful APIs that powered the app's core functionality, including product listings, user accounts, and checkout. I also implemented an advanced search system that allowed users to quickly find products using various filters and keywords. In addition, I enhanced the flow and efficiency of the existing catalog system, which enabled the app to handle a large number of products and categories more efficiently. These features have contributed to the app's success and user satisfaction.

Ghraoui Chocolates

I developed a RESTful API for Ghraoui, a leading confectionery company in the Middle East, to support their full e-commerce system. The API allows customers to browse and purchase products online, and integrates with the company's inventory and payment systems. I implemented advanced security measures to protect customer data, and optimized the API for performance and scalability. The API has enabled Ghraoui to expand their online sales and deliver a seamless shopping experience to their customers.

Villaggio Qatar

I developed a responsive, website for Villaggio Mall Qatar, one of the largest shopping centers in the Middle East. The website features a modern design and intuitive navigation, and allows customers to browse products from a variety of stores and brands. I implemented advanced search and filtering capabilities, and integrated the website with the mall's in-store inventory system. The website has received positive feedback from customers and has contributed to the mall's sales growth.

Squeeze

I worked on API development for Squeezemassage.com, a leading provider of on-demand massage services. I developed and maintained a set of RESTful APIs that powered the company's online booking system and integrated with their payment and scheduling systems. I enhanced the flow and functionality of the booking process, and optimized the APIs for performance and scalability. These improvements have contributed to the company's growth and customer satisfaction.

Sellpoints (Syndigo)

I worked on the Sellpoints admin APIs, a set of RESTful APIs that enabled the company's team of product managers to manage and publish interactive product content on the Sellpoints platform. Sellpoints is an e-commerce optimization platform that helps brands to engage their customers by telling product stories through interactive "hotspots" on retail product pages.

About Me

I am a full-stack developer with over 11 years of experience in the industry. I started my career working with the LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL, and PHP), and then transitioned into Node.js development. I am now an expert in Express, NestJs, Vue, Angular and have a strong understanding of modern web development technologies and best practices.

In my current role, I am responsible for developing and maintaining web applications and APIs. I enjoy working on projects that are challenging and require me to think creatively and critically. I am a strong problem-solver, and I am always looking for ways to improve and optimize my code.

In my free time, I enjoy staying up to date with the latest developments in web development and exploring new technologies. I also enjoy spending time with my family and participating in outdoor activities as well as watching movies.
